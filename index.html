We follow a structured 6-phase project management process: (1) Project Initiation - defining scope and objectives, (2) Concept Development - preliminary design and planning, (3) Pre-Feasibility - initial assessments, (4) Feasibility and Planning - detailed planning and budgeting, (5) Implementation and Execution - construction and installation, and (6) Project Close-out and Handover - final delivery and documentation. This ensures cost efficiency, quality assurance, and timely delivery.
